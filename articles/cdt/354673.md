decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc17b454.png" class="lazyload" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc18a896.png" class="lazyload" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc1e4161.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>有网友说看了这个视频很难过。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc20b12c.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>发了这个号，他的B站账号就被禁了。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc21a93e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这是B站的显示页面。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc238150.png" class="lazyload" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc24c045.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>可是就算没有了户晨风，依然还会有其他的网友在关注着底层人们，比如找活的康根长。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc266d7f.png" class="lazyload" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc272e7a.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如立交桥下的人们。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc29390e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如等着零活干的高江才。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc2b0420.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc2bcb53.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如一个馒头当晚饭的程世斌。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/03/post-693902-6412ebc2d6e91.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>不管怎么样，总会有人记住这些底层人们的面容，并记录下他们的生活状态吧，从而提醒着这个时代依然会有不完美之处，依然需要去努力改善。</p><p>B站把户晨风的账号封没了是一种态度，网友们的接力记录同样也是一种态度，这样的态度柔和而又坚韧，让人难忘。</p><p>而我呢，只想记录下这样的坚韧，不只是坚韧生活，也有坚韧记录。</p><div
