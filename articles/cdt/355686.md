tropopause)，环境温度相对恒定，大约-55C的样子。我们假设流浪气球的科技跟nasa用的SPB一样，都是closed&nbsp;system。由于PV=nRT，PV/T恒定。</p><p><img decoding="async" src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692889-63e90e0da4fc5." alt="Image"></p><p>根据NASA给的压强公式（单位kPa）</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="Image" decoding="async" alt="Image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692889-63e90e1a56320.png" class="lazyload"
