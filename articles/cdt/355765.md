href="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/copyright">详细版权说明</a>。</div></div></div><p>谁能写出最完美的《战狼3》剧本？</p><p>有人说，应该让冷锋来写；也有人说，龙小云更适合。</p><p>但在这个科幻的时代，何不给这部作品增添一丝赛博朋克的色彩？</p><p>最近爆火的聊天机器人ChatGPT，能否肩负起撰写《战狼3》的重任？</p><p>毕竟，他能用鲁迅的口吻撰写足以斩获诺奖的疫情文学。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b34416e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b354655.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>连核酸和阳性这种话题都能妙笔生花，ChatGPT的能力不容小觑。</p><p>但我担心的是，他是否只能驾驭鲁迅先生的文风？其他的风格样式，是否也能行云流水？</p><p>比如笑话。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b360029.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如诗句。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b36b53f.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如思想汇报。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b37e5e9.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>比如老胡体。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b39118f.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>最后，我也没忘记测试一下ChatGPT屁股歪不歪。毕竟要是写剧本的时候夹带了私货，那可就不好了。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b39b5a1.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>检查完毕，看这个回答，感觉可以归到外国友人的行列，那我们就开整。</p><p>剧本名自然是《战狼3》，角色则是冷锋、龙小云和拜登。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b3abb69.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>怎么说呢，这种上世纪八十年代谍战风的台词倒不是最大的问题，冷锋和拜登同仇敌忾，恐怕才是这个剧本的病根。</p><p>我顿悟，人物关系很重要，于是修改。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b3be66b.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>不得不说，这次感觉味儿冲多了。</p><p>但是人物还是过于脸谱化，这不像是一个剧本，更像是一个大纲。</p><p>细节在哪里，情节在哪里，人物的血肉又在哪里？</p><p>于是我再度修改。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b3d342f.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b3e565f.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b3ef669." alt="image"></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b408024.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这次的剧本已经很成熟，但感觉还是仅仅局限于冷锋夫妇与拜登的交锋。</p><p>他们的动机是什么？战争的目的是什么？故事的主线是什么？</p><p>于是我再度修改。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b41c68e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b42e118.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b43f973.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/02/post-692810-63e626b45128b.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>错综复杂的剧情关系比肩东野圭吾的小说，鸿笔丽藻的一词一句不输莎士比亚的戏剧。</p><p>怕是吴京本人看了都要惊呼一声：任何邪恶终将绳之以法！</p><p>建议《战狼3》剧组立刻给我打钱，否则我就真要卖这剧本卖给拜登了。</p><p>备注：本文纯属虚构，如果日后剧本雷同，那应该是真的找我当编剧了。</p><div
