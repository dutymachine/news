Which, I suspect, is <strong>the way that the virus started in Wuhan,</strong> to be honest. It makes no sense that this virus popped out of nowhere. It’s bullsh*t,” he said.</p><p>总的来讲，如果你选择相信这篇报道，那你就不能仅仅相信辉瑞制作了新冠病毒，也得相信virus started in Wuhan.</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image"
