title="**这里**"><strong>这里</strong></a>。</p><p>距离小花梅第一次被全国网友知道的时候，已经快过去一年了，有人已经淡忘了她，但也有人一直记得她，并牵挂着她。</p><p>这里就不免要佩服一下李庄，他不但一直记得小花梅，还想着去看到她，只是没想到，连去看小花梅这件事本身，都会成为一个历险记。</p><p>李庄是受网友们的委托而去看小花梅的。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32a3ae3.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>他是昨天去董集村的。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32b1f8c.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>李庄眼里的村干部，有几分董志民的范儿。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32bed6f.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>面对李庄的质问，村干部选择沉默。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32cb593.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>登记完后，还是不让进村。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32d8682.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这个冬天，董集村防外人如防贼，每个人都要问一遍。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32e372e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这是当地村干部与上级的交涉。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f32f08ac.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>报了警，一刻钟也没动静。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f3309a98.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>直到半个小时后，才有警员赶过来。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f33179e9.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>当地警员说，任何外人去了董集村，都要在县里报备。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f3325c06.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这就是李庄昨天写于董集村的。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f3331b6e.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>就是这个人拦着不让开进去。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f333f657.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这就是登记名单。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f334e372.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这些人在负责盘问。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f335f36d.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>其中还有个戴眼镜的。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f336b792.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>当地警员正在汇报交涉。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2023/01/post-691938-63bf0f337b7a5.png" class="lazyload" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAAAAACH5BAEKAAEALAAAAAABAAEAAAICTAEAOw=="></p><p>这就是昨天李庄在董集村所遭遇的事，我想记录下来让大家看到。</p><p><img decoding="async" alt="image"
