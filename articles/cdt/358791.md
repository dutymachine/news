src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb19cd3.png" alt="image"></p><p>这些都是石家庄网友的亲身体验。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb236fb.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb2c14d.png" src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb50fdb.png" alt="image"></p><p>当地教育部门下发了这样的通知。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb6090f.png" alt="image"></p><p>可有家长不敢让自己的孩子返校。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb72f96.png" alt="image"></p><p>另一个小区家长们也不让自己的孩子返校。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb88b50.png" alt="image"></p><p>接下来就真的精彩了，有网友曝出了孩子不返校的内幕。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eb95b8b.png" alt="image"></p><p>还有网友发现了这个。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eba3fdb.png" alt="image"></p><p>可是不管家长们怎么想怎么报，石家庄社区已经下发了这样的通知。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ebb3049.png" alt="image"></p><p>我查了一下，这两个的确都在石家庄。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ebc122c.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ebcb13f.png" alt="image"></p><p>裕华区则发布了这样的通知。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ebd892c.png" alt="image"></p><p>于是有网友这样感叹。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ebe8879.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友则发现了这个。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec060dd.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友在表示害怕和担心，毕竟家人和朋友都在石家庄。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec138cb.png" alt="image"></p><p>也有网友拿出行动了，比如去买莲花。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec43114.png" alt="image"></p><p>底下的网友可不乐意了，于是开始反唇相讥。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec51714.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec5fcdb.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友说自己还是支持放开的，毕竟绝大多数人都要考虑谋生的问题。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec6b908.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友则说自己失去了一项福利。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec80c97.png" alt="image"></p><p>之所以会这样说，是因为他们发现了这个。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec91043.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友则想到了这样一个问题。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ec9d0a4.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友愤愤不平地表达了自己的观点。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ecb8ab7.png" alt="image"></p><p>当然放开这事也不是一帆风顺，总会有波折的，昨天桥西区说要恢复核检，底下的评论一片欢呼。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ecc53d9.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458eccf985.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ecdbe0e.png" alt="image"></p><p>这样的评论还挺多的。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ece9ad0.png" alt="image"></p><p>这是桥西区网友的朋友圈。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed08173.png" alt="image"></p><p>但很快又传来这样的消息。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed1126f.png" alt="image"></p><p>面对这样的反转再反转，网友们说自己的心脏受不了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed1b316.png" alt="image"></p><p>也有网友提出这样的建议。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed28b94.png" alt="image"></p><p>当然更多的石家庄网友在为放开而高兴，至少可以开心的看表演了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed397dc.png" alt="image"></p><p>这是他们脸上的笑容。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed58e0c.png" alt="image"></p><p>当然也会有失落者，比如小区的保安。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/11/post-689784-637458ed63579.png" alt="image"></p><p>这就是刚刚过去的24小时里，石家庄围绕着放开与否，所上演的一幕幕精彩戏份，我还是想写出来让大家看到。</p><p>无论如何，让普通人过得好一点，让他们少受一点折腾，多过几天安生日子总是不会错的，也是我们社会管理者应该去努力做到的。</p><div class="addtoany_share_save_container addtoany_content
