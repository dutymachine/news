src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/image-1664274648168.png" alt="file"></p><p>于是有网友忍不住感叹。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c25df4c.png" alt="image"></p><p>还有网友觉得这部电影已经完整了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c267ef0.png" alt="imagev"></p><p>平台们已经搜索不到关于它的消息了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c27082b.png" alt="image"></p><p>另一个平台也是这样。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c27bc2d.png" alt="imagev"></p><p>与此同时，东八区的先生们也下架了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c289c4e.png" alt="imagev"></p><p>底下的网友们一片欢腾。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c295bad.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友表示不满意了，说东八区的立场没问题，不应下架。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2a525a.png" alt="image"></p><p>有网友则认为它的评分太低，没有上架的资格。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2add79.png" alt="image"></p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2bd91c.png" alt="image"></p><p>对此，有网友说得很明白了，标准不能随心所欲。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2cbe96.png" alt="image"></p><p>还有网友说这样玩下去就要玩废了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2dab39.png" alt="image"></p><p>在网友看来，现在这上架又下架就像一场儿戏。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c2ecfdc.png" alt="image"></p><p>于是网友们开始在想这个事情了。</p><p><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2022/09/post-687581-6332d0c305372.png" alt="image"></p><p>这就是我们当下所面临的现实，创作的好要下架，创作的不好也要下架，于是人们要茫然了，到底要怎么创作呢？</p><div
