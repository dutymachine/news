id="easy-footnote-25-672723" class="easy-footnote-margin-adjust"></span><span class="easy-footnote"><a href="#easy-footnote-bottom-25-672723" title="Zhou Xueguang, The institutional logic of href="#easy-footnote-bottom-27-672723" title="Zhou Yongkun, ‘The history and evolution of the PLAC’’ [周永坤: 政法委的历史与演变], Yanghuang Chunqiu [炎黄春秋] via Aisixiang [爱思想], 10 September 2012."><sup>&nbsp;[27]&nbsp;</sup></a></span>在20世纪50年代和60年代的动荡之后，政法委退居二线，并在1988年被解散。但在天安门事件后，政法委在1990年被重启。<span id="easy-footnote-28-672723" class="easy-footnote-margin-adjust"></span><span class="easy-footnote"><a href="#easy-footnote-bottom-28-672723" title="Qin Qianhong [秦前红], ‘The real problem that Qiao Shi was reflecting upon’ [乔石思考的真问题], Sina News [新浪新闻], 15 June 2015."><sup>&nbsp;[28]&nbsp;</sup></a></span>许多学者认为，政法委的存在就意味着中国共产党和政府之间并不存在有意义的切割。<span id="easy-footnote-29-672723" class="easy-footnote-margin-adjust"></span><span class="easy-footnote"><a
