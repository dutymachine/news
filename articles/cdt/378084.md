[杂谈君｜央视呼吁停止污名化“伞兵” 百度贴吧疑似解禁“傻逼”一词](https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/670491.html)
------

<p>9月3日，央视网青年官方微博账号在微博发文，呼吁停止污名化“伞兵”。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946977555.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946977555.png" alt="file"></p><p>这条微博一经发出就受到了众多媒体和大V的转发支持，新华社也撰文声援。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946987195.png" alt="imgv"></noscript><img class="lazyload" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946987195.png" alt="imgv"></p><p>但微博网友们除了响应号召之外，也犀利地指出“伞兵”这个词被借用污化的根本原因。</p><p><noscript><img src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946999757.png" alt="file"></noscript><img class="lazyload" src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E" data-src="https://chinadigitaltimes.net/chinese/files/2021/09/image-1630946999757.png" alt="file"></p><p>就在网友们思索接下来会是哪个词惨遭毒手被词义转化时，有些百度贴吧的用户发现，“傻逼”这个词居然在贴吧解禁了。</p><p><noscript><img src="data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20210%20140%22%3E%3C/svg%3E"
