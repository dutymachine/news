[中國官宣第三艘航母「福建號」氣球再入台灣上空](https://www.dw.com/zh/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E5%AE%98%E5%AE%A3%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%89%E8%89%98%E8%88%AA%E6%AF%8D%E3%80%8C%E7%A6%8F%E5%BB%BA%E8%99%9F%E3%80%8D%E6%B0%A3%E7%90%83%E5%86%8D%E5%85%A5%E5%8F%B0%E7%81%A3%E4%B8%8A%E7%A9%BA/a-67880401)
