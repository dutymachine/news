[美国批评以色列极右政治家言论“不负责任“](https://www.dw.com/zh/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E6%89%B9%E8%AF%84%E4%BB%A5%E8%89%B2%E5%88%97%E6%9E%81%E5%8F%B3%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB%E5%AE%B6%E8%A8%80%E8%AE%BA%E2%80%9C%E4%B8%8D%E8%B4%9F%E8%B4%A3%E4%BB%BB%E2%80%9C/a-67880877)
