[美國前國務卿季辛吉離世 享嵩壽100歲](https://www.dw.com/zh/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9C%8B%E5%89%8D%E5%9C%8B%E5%8B%99%E5%8D%BF%E5%AD%A3%E8%BE%9B%E5%90%89%E9%9B%A2%E4%B8%96%20%E4%BA%AB%E5%B5%A9%E5%A3%BD100%E6%AD%B2/a-67590702)
