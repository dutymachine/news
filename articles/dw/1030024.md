#999;">中韩关系在2023年持续紧张</small></div><p>（德国之声中文网）在<a href="https://api.dw.com/api/detail/article/65871711" src="https://images.weserv.nl/?url=static.dw.com/image/65872086_303.jpg" width="100%"><div><small style="color:
