[热热热！中国多地遭遇高温“烤验”](https://www.dw.com/zh/%E7%83%AD%E7%83%AD%E7%83%AD%EF%BC%81%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%A4%9A%E5%9C%B0%E9%81%AD%E9%81%87%E9%AB%98%E6%B8%A9%E2%80%9C%E7%83%A4%E9%AA%8C%E2%80%9D/a-62890113)
