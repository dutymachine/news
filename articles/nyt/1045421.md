href="https://www.nytimes.com/live/2022/04/05/world/ukraine-russia-war/a-red-cross-convoy-to-mariupol-has-stalled-the-organization-says" title="">红十字会疏散小组未能进入马里乌波尔</a>。</p></li><li><p><a
