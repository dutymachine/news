href="https://www.sb.gov.hk/eng/articles/letters_2021_06_18.html">警告函</a>，一些外国记者无法拿到这个英国前殖民地的工作<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.globaltimes.cn/page/202111/1238333.shtml" title="Link: https://www.globaltimes.cn/page/202111/1238333.shtml">他说</a>。</p><p><img src="https://images.weserv.nl/?url=static01.nyt.com/images/2021/12/29/world/29hongkong-02/merlin_199723803_ac95ab85-9d81-48a3-a3ac-fad77b7add39-master1050.jpg"><small style="color: href="https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20211025/hong-kong-civil-society/">香港记者协会</a>的主席，这个由大约500名本地记者组成的行业组织已经遭受审查。</p><p>保安局局长邓炳强曾在9月<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"
