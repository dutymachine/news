[美国从阿富汗撤军对台湾意味着什么](https://cn.nytimes.com/opinion/20210914/china-taiwan-afghanistan/)
------

<address>ORIANA SKYLAR MASTRO</address><time pudate="2021-09-14 11:01:01" datetime="2021-09-14 11:01:01">2021年9月14日</time><figure class="article-span-photo"><img src="https://images.weserv.nl/?url=static01.nyt.com/images/2021/09/13/opinion/13Mastro-02/merlin_187296951_f32b6c67-573c-4e26-bc97-8fc31ecb4c0f-master1050.jpg" width="1050" height="700"><figcaption> <cite>Ann Wang/Reuters</cite></figcaption></figure><section class="article-body"><p>有<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/china/2021-06-03/china-taiwan-war-temptation" title="Link: https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/china/2021-06-03/china-taiwan-war-temptation">许多理由</a>担心中国即将<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/asia-pacific/20210714/japan-taiwan-china-us/" title="Link: https://cn.nytimes.com/asia-pacific/20210714/japan-taiwan-china-us/">对台湾发动进攻</a>：中国加强了<a href="https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-58459128" rel="noopener noreferrer">空中活动</a>；<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://news.usni.org/2021/03/09/davidson-china-could-try-to-take-control-of-taiwan-in-next-six-years" title="Link: https://news.usni.org/2021/03/09/davidson-china-could-try-to-take-control-of-taiwan-in-next-six-years">五角大楼发出高调警告</a>；中国<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.cfr.org/backgrounder/chinas-modernizing-military">军事的快速现代化</a>；国家主席习近平不断升级的<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://asia.nikkei.com/Politics/Full-text-of-Xi-Jinping-s-speech-on-the-CCP-s-100th-anniversary" title="Link: https://asia.nikkei.com/Politics/Full-text-of-Xi-Jinping-s-speech-on-the-CCP-s-100th-anniversary">言论</a>。但美国从阿富汗撤军不是理由之一，尽管外交政策和军事圈子最近的激烈讨论正在这样暗示。</p><p>一些批评拜登总统从阿富汗撤军决定的人<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://nationalinterest.org/blog/buzz/america-failed-afghanistan-now-it-must-stand-taiwan-192461">认为</a>，这将让北京的<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-58312949" title="Link: https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-58312949">胆子更大</a>，因为撤军流露了软弱——不愿坚持到底、打赢战争——中国在决定是否进攻台湾时将考虑到这些因素。它认为台湾是其领土的一部分。</p><p>然而，实际情况是，美国从阿富汗撤军更有可能让中国的战争规划者踌躇，而不是推动他们对台湾使用武力。</p><p>中国共产党宣称的目标是“<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.12371.cn/2020/07/23/ARTI1595467453716357.shtml" title="Link: http://www.12371.cn/2020/07/23/ARTI1595467453716357.shtml">中华民族伟大复兴</a>”，即恢复中国作为世界强国的<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20180227/xi-jinping-thought-explained-a-new-ideology-for-a-new-era/" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2018/02/26/world/asia/xi-jinping-thought-explained-a-new-ideology-for-a-new-era.html">地位</a>。中国的领导人和思想家<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://muse.jhu.edu/book/64418">已经研究了</a>世界强国过去的兴衰史。他们早就明白，美国的遏制可能<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/14799855.2016.1157786" title="Link: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/14799855.2016.1157786">阻止中国成为一个强国</a>。</p><p>对北京来说幸运的是，阿富汗战争——以及美国在伊拉克和其他中东地区的不幸遭遇——已让华盛顿的注意力分散了20年。在中国从北京到意大利<a href="https://www.triesteallnews.it/2019/11/porto-di-trieste-protagonista-del-made-in-italy-in-cina-siglato-accordo/" rel="noopener noreferrer" title="Link: https://www.triesteallnews.it/2019/11/porto-di-trieste-protagonista-del-made-in-italy-in-cina-siglato-accordo/">里雅斯特</a>沿途修建公路和港口、刺激本国的经济发展、扩大本国地缘政治影响力的时候，美国正在把大量资金投入到反恐战争中。当北京<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/asia-pacific/20180921/south-china-sea-navy/" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2018/09/20/world/asia/south-china-sea-navy.html">在南中国海修建几十平方公里的军事基地</a>、增强本国<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://media.defense.gov/2020/Sep/01/2002488689/-1/-1/1/2020-DOD-CHINA-MILITARY-POWER-REPORT-FINAL.PDF" title="Link: https://media.defense.gov/2020/Sep/01/2002488689/-1/-1/1/2020-DOD-CHINA-MILITARY-POWER-REPORT-FINAL.PDF">精准打击能力</a>的时候，美国军队正在与叛乱分子作战，拆除简易爆炸装置。</p><p>习近平和他的前任们之所以能够逐渐增强中国的影响力、<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/opinions/2019/06/27/chinas-efforts-undermine-democracy-are-expanding-worldwide/">削弱国际规范</a>、<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.wsj.com/articles/how-china-is-taking-over-international-organizations-one-vote-at-a-time-11601397208" title="Link: https://www.wsj.com/articles/how-china-is-taking-over-international-organizations-one-vote-at-a-time-11601397208">拉拢</a>国际组织、扩大他们<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.inquirer.net/specials/exclusive-china-militarization-south-china-sea/">控制的领土</a>，部分得益于阿富汗战争，而且这些都是在美国没有以任何有意义的方式阻挠他们的计划时发生的，从许多方面来看，这只是偶然的运气。</p><p>而阿富汗战争的结束可能会让这些好日子（中共称之为“<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.china.org.cn/english/2002/Nov/49107.htm" title="Link: http://www.china.org.cn/english/2002/Nov/49107.htm">重要战略机遇期</a>”）突然消失。的确，美国总统曾在过去10年里试图重新回到亚洲的角逐中来，尽管阿富汗战争仍在继续。贝拉克·奥巴马(Barack Obama)曾在2011年宣布，我们会<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.brookings.edu/articles/the-american-pivot-to-asia/" title="Link: https://www.brookings.edu/articles/the-american-pivot-to-asia/">把重心转向亚洲</a>。唐纳德·特朗普(Donald Trump)的国家安全团队曾把与中国的大国竞争作为首要任务。</p><p>但他们都只是说说而已，<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/asia/2021-08-11/america-still-needs-rebalance-asia">没有付出多少行动</a>。从阿富汗撤军表明，拜登的确是在重新调整他的国家安全优先事项——他甚至将“<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/speeches-remarks/2021/07/08/remarks-by-president-biden-on-the-drawdown-of-u-s-forces-in-afghanistan/" title="Link: https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/speeches-remarks/2021/07/08/remarks-by-president-biden-on-the-drawdown-of-u-s-forces-in-afghanistan/">集中力量加强美国的核心实力，以在与中国的战略竞争中交锋</a>”列为撤军的原因之一。</p><p>这种重新调整来得一点也不早。中国在南中国海的<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.prcleader.org/mastro" title="Link: https://www.prcleader.org/mastro">扩张和军事化</a>、与印度发生的致命<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.wsj.com/articles/india-china-himalaya-border-conflict-11592329619">冲突</a>、在香港的<a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2021/06/23/briefing/china-hong-kong-apple-daily-closure.html">镇压</a>和在新疆的<a href="https://www.nytimes.com/zh/2019/11/16/world/asia/xinjiang-documents-chinese.html" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2019/11/16/world/asia/china-xinjiang-documents.html">压制</a>，都表明中国越来越自信、越来越咄咄逼人。特别是，中国在台湾周围的军事活动<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.npr.org/2021/06/15/1006921645/china-sends-a-record-28-military-planes-into-airspace-controlled-by-taiwan" title="Link: https://www.npr.org/2021/06/15/1006921645/china-sends-a-record-28-military-planes-into-airspace-controlled-by-taiwan">激增</a>——2020年，中国入侵台湾领空的次数创下了纪录。军事演习的复杂程度和规模也<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://uploads.fas.org/2020/08/ADIZ-Report.pdf" title="Link: https://uploads.fas.org/2020/08/ADIZ-Report.pdf">在增加</a>。与这些升级同时而来的是<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20210701/china-communist-party-anniversary/" title="Link: https://cn.nytimes.com/china/20210701/china-communist-party-anniversary/">习近平最近的警告</a>，他说，任何妄想欺负中国的外来势力都将“碰得头破血流”，任何走向“台独”的努力都会被“坚决粉碎”。</p><p>美国对台湾的政策是“战略模糊”，即不明确承诺保护台湾不受中国攻击。在当前紧张的环境中，美国的政策制定者和专家们正在拼命考虑如何让美国对台湾的<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/united-states/american-support-taiwan-must-be-unambiguous">承诺</a>更<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/china/2021-08-09/strait-emergency">可信</a>，并<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/china/2021-06-03/china-taiwan-war-temptation" title="Link: https://www.foreignaffairs.com/articles/china/2021-06-03/china-taiwan-war-temptation">加大针对中国的整体军事威慑</a>。最近一份向台湾出售<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.scmp.com/news/world/united-states-canada/article/3143859/biden-administration-approves-its-first-arms-sale">7.5亿美元武器</a>的提案就是这些努力的一部分，还有<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.scmp.com/news/china/diplomacy/article/3144998/biden-democracy-summit-invitation-taiwan-risks-crisis-china-us">邀请</a>台湾参加一个民主峰会的讨论，这无疑会激怒北京。</p><p>一些人<a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/opinions/2021/08/17/condoleezza-rice-afghans-didnt-choose-taliban/" rel="noopener noreferrer">认为</a>，美国从阿富汗撤军削弱了美国向台湾发出支持信号的努力。从表面上看，美国的撤军似乎对中国称之为“武统”的前景是件好事。的确，这正是中国的民族主义报纸《环球时报》兜售的信息：<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://world.huanqiu.com/article/44O06d2t4fk">美国将抛弃台湾</a>，就像美国以前抛弃了越南、如今抛弃了阿富汗那样。</p><p>然而，美国从阿富汗撤军给中国自己的后院带来了安全问题，可能会分散中国与美国竞争的注意力。北京保护自己全球利益的策略一方面是依靠<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.rand.org/news/press/2018/03/27.html" title="Link: https://www.rand.org/news/press/2018/03/27.html">东道国安全部队</a>和<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.scmp.com/news/china/diplomacy/article/3104876/china-relies-private-security-firms-keep-workers-and-projects" title="Link: https://www.scmp.com/news/china/diplomacy/article/3104876/china-relies-private-security-firms-keep-workers-and-projects">私人安全承包商</a>，另一方面是搭其他国家军事存在的<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://cwp.sipa.columbia.edu/news/china-and-free-rider-problem-exploring-case-energy-security-cwp-alumni-andrew-kennedy" title="Link: https://cwp.sipa.columbia.edu/news/china-and-free-rider-problem-exploring-case-energy-security-cwp-alumni-andrew-kennedy">便车</a>。分析人士已得出<a href="https://www.rand.org/pubs/research_reports/RR2271.html" rel="noopener noreferrer">结论</a>，中国不太可能向美国那样依赖自己的军事力量来保护本国的海外利益。北京似乎要竭力避免重犯华盛顿犯过的错误，那就是，在推进外交政策目标上过度依赖<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.fmprc.gov.cn/mfa_eng/zxxx_662805/t1905753.shtml">海外的军事干预</a>。</p><p>现在，阿富汗将不再有可靠的安全存在，这个中国有<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/opinion/20210823/china-afghanistan-taliban/" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2021/08/20/opinion/china-afghanistan-taliban.html">重大经济和商业利益</a>的地区毫无疑问会出现更广泛的不稳定。中国领导人还担心，阿富汗的冲突可能会<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="http://www.whb.cn/zhuzhan/rd/20210830/421778.html">漫过边境</a>蔓延到新疆，北京在新疆的镇压做法已经引起了国际社会的广泛谴责。</p><p>实际情况是，美国在阿富汗停留的时间比大多数人预期的长得多。这搅乱了中国对美国在台湾危机中会怎么做的计算，因为北京的普遍信念是，<a href="https://www.nytimes.com/1993/10/25/world/details-of-us-raid-in-somalia-success-so-near-a-loss-so-deep.html">索马里遗留下来的痛苦</a>将阻止华盛顿向台北提供援助。</p><p>但美国对阿富汗和伊拉克的干预引起了人们对这些假设的怀疑。台湾有相当大规模的经济和<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.reuters.com/world/china/taiwan-seeks-again-reassure-us-over-tackling-chip-shortage-2021-08-24/">半导体产业</a>，对美国具有重要战略意义。美国在东亚的实力和影响力依赖于自己在该地区的盟友和军事基地，以及美国作为首选安全伙伴的更广泛角色。如果台湾受到中国攻击，包括美国盟友在内的许多国家将视之为中国世界秩序到来的标志。相比之下，阿富汗的战略意义不那么重要，但美国还在那里停留了20年。</p><p>这对北京对台湾的任何图谋来说，都不是好兆头。</p><p>诚然，考虑到台湾与大陆的距离，中国会受益于主场优势，而且北京也有<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.defenseone.com/ideas/2021/03/taiwan-wants-more-missiles-s-not-bad-thing/172887/">比台湾大得多</a>的武器库。与美国国内对又一次干预的支持相比，中国也可能在任何冲突中得到更多国内公众的支持。</p><p>但如果中国有任何希望赢得一场跨海峡战争的话，中国军队就必须<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.aei.org/articles/the-taiwan-temptation/">快速行动</a>，让美国<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://news.yahoo.com/were-going-to-lose-fast-us-air-force-held-a-war-game-that-started-with-a-chinese-biological-attack-170003936.html?__twitter_impression=true" title="Link: https://news.yahoo.com/were-going-to-lose-fast-us-air-force-held-a-war-game-that-started-with-a-chinese-biological-attack-170003936.html?__twitter_impression=true">来不及作出反应</a>。<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.dw.com/zh/客座评论中共对美的持久战/a-55370231">中国的规划者</a>知道，战争持续的时间越长，美国的优势就越大。中国的生产和制造中心都可能成为美国的目标，但美国的本土相对<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.rand.org/content/dam/rand/pubs/research_reports/RR1100/RR1140/RAND_RR1140.pdf">安全</a>，中国用常规武器很难打美国。中国依赖<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://www.forbes.com/sites/judeclemente/2019/10/17/china-is-the-worlds-largest-oil--gas-importer/">外部</a>石油和天然气来源的程度要高得多，因此更容易受美国切断供应的影响。</p><p>而且中国经济将遭受更大的损失：由于战争将发生在亚洲，那里的贸易势必会中断。美国只需要坚持较短的时间，不需要20年，这些因素就会发挥作用。</p><p>拜登与习近平上周四通电话时暗示了这个重大利害关系——双方“<a href="https://cn.nytimes.com/usa/20210910/biden-xi-china/" title="Link: https://www.nytimes.com/2021/09/10/us/politics/biden-xi-china.html">讨论了双方确保竞争不转向冲突的责任</a>”（据白宫公布的版本）。</p><p>中国领导人<a rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" href="https://uploads.fas.org/2020/08/ADIZ-Report.pdf">已经预料到</a>与拜登政府的关系会紧张。现在他们面临的事实是，美国可能有意愿也有资源来抵抗中国的攻击，即使这意味着战争。</p><p>因此，尽管可能有种种理由反对结束阿富汗战争，但撤军对中国围绕台湾考量的影响不是，也不应该是其中的一个理由。</p></section><footer
