[中国央行调查：四季度货币政策感受指数和贷款总体需求指数均环比下降](https://cn.reuters.com/article/china-cen-survey-plcy-loan-index-1231-idCNKBS2JA0BR)
------

<div><i>2021-12-31T08:05:38Z</i></div><p>路透北京12月31日 - 中国央行周五发布四季度银行家调查显示，当季货币政策感受指数为54.1%，比上季下降0.2个百分点；贷款总体需求指数为67.7%，比上季下降0.6个百分点。</p><p>央行网站发布的调查 <a href="http://www.pbc.gov.cn/goutongjiaoliu/113456/113469/4435980/2021123016433889608.pdf">here</a>并显示，四季度银行家宏观经济热度指数35.3%，比上季下降3.4个百分点；企业家宏观经济热度指数为37.3%，比上季下降2.6个百分点。</p><p>四季度储户调查 <a href="http://www.pbc.gov.cn/goutongjiaoliu/113456/113469/4435975/2021123016430581471.pdf">here</a>则显示，当季就业感受指数为41.4%，比上季上升0.6个百分点；下季度物价预期指数为63.8%，比上季上升5.1个百分点。</p><p>企业家调查 <a href="http://www.pbc.gov.cn/goutongjiaoliu/113456/113469/4435985/2021123016440868537.pdf%E8%BF%98%E6%98%BE%E7%A4%BA%EF%BC%8C%E5%8E%9F%E6%9D%90%E6%96%99%E8%B4%AD%E8%BF%9B%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC%E6%84%9F%E5%8F%97%E6%8C%87%E6%95%B0%E4%B8%BA70.6%25%EF%BC%8C%E6%AF%94%E4%B8%8A%E5%AD%A3%E4%B8%8B%E9%99%8D1.4%E4%B8%AA%E7%99%BE%E5%88%86%E7%82%B9%EF%BC%8C%E6%AF%94%E5%8E%BB%E5%B9%B4%E5%90%8C%E6%9C%9F%E6%8F%90%E9%AB%986.4%E4%B8%AA%E7%99%BE%E5%88%86%E7%82%B9%EF%BC%9B%E5%87%BA%E5%8F%A3%E8%AE%A2%E5%8D%95%E6%8C%87%E6%95%B0%E4%B8%BA48.7%25%EF%BC%8C%E6%AF%94%E4%B8%8A%E5%AD%A3%E4%B8%8B%E9%99%8D0.7%E4%B8%AA%E7%99%BE%E5%88%86%E7%82%B9%EF%BC%8C%E6%AF%94%E5%8E%BB%E5%B9%B4%E5%90%8C%E6%9C%9F%E6%8F%90%E9%AB%982.3%E4%B8%AA%E7%99%BE%E5%88%86%E7%82%B9%EF%BC%9B%E4%BA%A7%E5%93%81%E9%94%80%E5%94%AE%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC%E6%84%9F%E5%8F%97%E6%8C%87%E6%95%B0%E4%B8%BA57.9%25%EF%BC%8C%E6%AF%94%E4%B8%8A%E5%AD%A3%E4%B8%8B%E9%99%8D0.3%E4%B8%AA%E7%99%BE%E5%88%86%E7%82%B9%E3%80%82%EF%BC%88%E5%AE%8C%EF%BC%89">here</a></p>
