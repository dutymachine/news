[台湾上校遭对岸吸收 着军服签署“投降承诺书”](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/gangtai/hcm2-11232022065523.html)
------

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">在台海情势紧张之际，台湾的陆军步训部上校主任向德恩涉遭媒体记者邵维强吸收，以每月新台币</span><span style="font-weight: 300;">4</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">万元</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">。检调依贪污等罪将向德恩起诉求刑</span><span style="font-weight: 300;">12</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年。分析指，检方未以</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">“</span><span style="font-weight: style="font-weight: 300;">35</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">年，现为陆军步兵训练指挥部作战研究发展室上校主任研究教官，涉嫌在</span><span style="font-weight: 300;">2019</span><span style="font-weight:
