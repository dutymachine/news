@wenjun7011发出两张图片，一张姓名“肖才初”的护照被剪一角，一张是他的身份证被剪一角。</p><p><span class="discreet"><span><span class="searchresultdate"></span></span></span></p><p><figure class="image-richtext image-inline captioned"
