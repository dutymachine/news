[加拿大多族裔百馀人在温哥华发起抵制北京冬奥的呼声](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/junshiwaijiao/lf-11222021132109.html)
------

href="https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/renquanfazhi/lf-05262021141250.html"><strong>抵制北京奥运
