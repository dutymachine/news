[专访宋永毅：从政治心理的角度管窥文革史和今日中国政治](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/zhengzhi/wy-11102021143710.html)

src="https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/zhengzhi/wy-11102021143710.html/wy1111.jpg/@@images/e7726f7b-c3cf-4a68-96bc-eba9ed0fee18.jpeg" title="wy1111.jpg" width="620"/><figcaption
