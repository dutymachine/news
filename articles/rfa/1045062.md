[南非骚乱华人成抢掠目标     学者 : 华商在非洲形象差所致](https://www.rfa.org/mandarin/yataibaodao/junshiwaijiao/ql2-07152021055145.html)
------

<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">南非发生的大规模骚乱持续，已经导致</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">72</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">人丧生，</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">1200</span><span 400;">7</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">14</span><span style="font-weight: style="font-weight: 400;">月</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">13</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">日，南非警察部发言人利兰祖</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">·</span><span style="font-weight:
