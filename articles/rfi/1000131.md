[摩洛哥为重建地震灾区拨巨款](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230922-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E6%91%A9%E6%B4%9B%E5%93%A5%E4%B8%BA%E9%87%8D%E5%BB%BA%E5%9C%B0%E9%9C%87%E7%81%BE%E5%8C%BA%E6%8B%A8%E5%B7%A8%E6%AC%BE)
