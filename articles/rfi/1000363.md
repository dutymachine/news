[泄漏儿童数据 欧盟重罚抖音](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20230915-%E6%B3%84%E6%BC%8F%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE-%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E9%87%8D%E7%BD%9A%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3)

<div>15/09/2023 - 17:46</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/c0d60502-c7e6-11ed-a4a1-005056bf30b7/w:1280/p:16x9/AP23080375686495.jpg"><p><strong>欧洲联盟资料隐私监管机构今天基于惩罚儿童数据泄露，对中国拥有的社群媒体平台抖音TikTok开罚3亿4500万欧元。        </strong></p><div><p>据中央社引述消息称，爱尔兰资料保护委员会（Data Protection Commission, DPC）发表声明指出，在进行两年调查后，发现TikTok违反相关规定，因此对抖音处以“行政罚款”。</p><p>爱尔兰资料保护委员会是欧盟地区主要的资料隐私监管机构，负责监督欧盟通用资料保护规则（GDPR）的落实。该机构于2021年9月开始调查TikTok是否遵守GDPR，已给予TikTok3个月时间使抖音处理方式符合规定。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9w5S.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E6%25AC%25A7%25E6%25B4%25B2%2F20230915-%25E6%25B3%2584%25E6%25BC%258F%25E5%2584%25BF%25E7%25AB%25A5%25E6%2595%25B0%25E6%258D%25AE-%25E6%25AC%25A7%25E7%259B%259F%25E9%2587%258D%25E7%25BD%259A%25E6%258A%2596%25E9%259F%25B3&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E6%B3%84%E6%BC%8F%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%20%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E9%87%8D%E7%BD%9A%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9w5S.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E6%B3%84%E6%BC%8F%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%20%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E9%87%8D%E7%BD%9A%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9w5S.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9w5S.T&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E6%B3%84%E6%BC%8F%E5%84%BF%E7%AB%A5%E6%95%B0%E6%8D%AE%20%E6%AC%A7%E7%9B%9F%E9%87%8D%E7%BD%9A%E6%8A%96%E9%9F%B3&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
