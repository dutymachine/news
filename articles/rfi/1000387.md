[习近平向海青论坛致贺信 称两岸关系牢握在中国人手中](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230915-%E4%B9%A0%E8%BF%91%E5%B9%B3%E5%90%91%E6%B5%B7%E9%9D%92%E8%AE%BA%E5%9D%9B%E8%87%B4%E8%B4%BA%E4%BF%A1-%E7%A7%B0%E4%B8%A4%E5%B2%B8%E5%85%B3%E7%B3%BB%E7%89%A2%E6%8F%A1%E5%9C%A8%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E6%89%8B%E4%B8%AD)
