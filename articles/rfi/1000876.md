[普京称将很快与习近平会面](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20230901-%E6%99%AE%E4%BA%AC%E7%A7%B0%E5%B0%86%E5%BE%88%E5%BF%AB%E4%B8%8E%E4%B9%A0%E8%BF%91%E5%B9%B3%E4%BC%9A%E9%9D%A2)
------
