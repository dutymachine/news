[冰岛宣布恢复捕猎鲸鱼](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%94%9F%E6%80%81/20230901-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E5%86%B0%E5%B2%9B%E5%AE%A3%E5%B8%83%E6%81%A2%E5%A4%8D%E6%8D%95%E7%8C%8E%E9%B2%B8%E9%B1%BC)
