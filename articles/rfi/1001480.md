[日美韩确认三国合作重要](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20230816-%E6%97%A5%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9%E7%A1%AE%E8%AE%A4%E4%B8%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C%E9%87%8D%E8%A6%81)
------

<div>16/08/2023 - 12:01</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/27c7faf2-15e5-11ea-af8e-005056a99247/w:1280/p:16x9/mrh.jpg"><p><strong>日本外相林芳正15日与美国国务卿布林肯、韩国外长朴振举行在线会谈，一致认为日美韩三国的合作对于应对朝鲜、维护地区及国际社会的和平稳定、实现“自由开放的印度太平洋”而言十分重要。该外长会谈把18日将于美国举行的日美韩首脑会谈视为三国合作的“历史性机遇”，为此梳理了论点。        </strong></p><div><p>日美韩外长确认三国合作重要性.据共同社今天透露，日本首相岸田文雄为出席首脑会谈将于17日访美。</p><p>美国国务卿布林肯在会谈后的记者会上称，他认为日美韩加强合作对地区的和平稳定、以及实现朝鲜半岛无核化很重要。</p><p>据共同社，18日的首脑会谈由美国总统拜登邀请岸田、韩国总统尹锡悦在华盛顿近郊的总统庄园戴维营举行。这将是在国际会议以外的场合，日美韩首脑第一次为举行三边会谈而聚首。预计三国首脑将在联合记者会上发表联合文件。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9qin.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25BA%259A%25E6%25B4%25B2%2F20230816-%25E6%2597%25A5%25E7%25BE%258E%25E9%259F%25A9%25E7%25A1%25AE%25E8%25AE%25A4%25E4%25B8%2589%25E5%259B%25BD%25E5%2590%2588%25E4%25BD%259C%25E9%2587%258D%25E8%25A6%2581&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E6%97%A5%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9%E7%A1%AE%E8%AE%A4%E4%B8%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C%E9%87%8D%E8%A6%81%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9qin.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E6%97%A5%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9%E7%A1%AE%E8%AE%A4%E4%B8%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C%E9%87%8D%E8%A6%81%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9qin.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9qin.T&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E6%97%A5%E7%BE%8E%E9%9F%A9%E7%A1%AE%E8%AE%A4%E4%B8%89%E5%9B%BD%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C%E9%87%8D%E8%A6%81&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
