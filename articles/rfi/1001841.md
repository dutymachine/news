[美国五角大楼与中国外交部罕见高官对话](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20230803-%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%94%E8%A7%92%E5%A4%A7%E6%A5%BC%E4%B8%8E%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%A4%96%E4%BA%A4%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%95%E8%A7%81%E9%AB%98%E5%AE%98%E5%AF%B9%E8%AF%9D)
