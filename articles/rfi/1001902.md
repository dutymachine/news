[北京等地暴雨酿灾 赖清德和陆委会表达慰问关怀](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE/20230801-%E5%8C%97%E4%BA%AC%E7%AD%89%E5%9C%B0%E6%9A%B4%E9%9B%A8%E9%85%BF%E7%81%BE-%E8%B5%96%E6%B8%85%E5%BE%B7%E5%92%8C%E9%99%86%E5%A7%94%E4%BC%9A%E8%A1%A8%E8%BE%BE%E6%85%B0%E9%97%AE%E5%85%B3%E6%80%80)
