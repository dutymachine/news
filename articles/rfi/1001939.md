[尼日尔:西共体不排除军事选项](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230731-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E5%B0%BC%E6%97%A5%E5%B0%94-%E8%A5%BF%E5%85%B1%E4%BD%93%E4%B8%8D%E6%8E%92%E9%99%A4%E5%86%9B%E4%BA%8B%E9%80%89%E9%A1%B9)
