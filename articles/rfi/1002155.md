><span><span><span>”</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>，因为</span></span></span></span></span></span></span>拜登<span><span><span><span><span><span><span>政府仍在努力敲定交付<span>战机</span>和培训乌克兰飞行员的计划。该官员补充说，美国正在努力让乌克兰“</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>尽快</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>”</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>获得</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>F-16战机，但这需要一些时间。<span>约翰</span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span>·柯比</span>和这位官员都没有具体说明哪些国家将<span>提供</span>F-16 </span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span>月</span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span> 12
