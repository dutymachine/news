Wormuth）。如果弗朗切蒂能够成功获得确认，作为美国海军第一位女统帅，她将标志着又有一项历史载入了美军史册。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>作为美军总司令，拜登周五还宣布了其它几个海军高级职位的提名</span></span></span><span><span><span>，包括海军作战部副部长人选。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>拜登在声明中说，“美国人民支持我们的军队及其家人”，并表示，“我敦促参议院尽快批准所有杰出的军事提名</span></span></span><span><span><span>人”。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><div
