</span><span>13 日</span><span>星期四晚上举行的烟花</span><span>表演。</span><span>多个城市或</span><span>在前或跟着</span><span>同样</span><span>也</span><span>取消</span><span>当晚传统上的国庆放烟火活动。</span><span>此外，</span></span><span><span>内政部长表示，国庆日</span><span>加上接着是周末的几天</span><span>的</span><span>骚乱</span><span>爆发</span><span>危险高峰，</span><span>维安工作，将出动130000名警察和宪兵。</span><span>与此同时，</span><span>爱丽舍宫</span><span>总统府发言人宣布</span><span>，</span><span>总统</span><span>马克龙</span><span>今年</span><span>7月14日</span><span>国庆日</span><span>当天</span><span>，将不发表演说，</span><span>等</span><span>国过几天才</span><span>向法国人民讲话。</span></span><span> </span></p><p </span><span>日晚上的庆祝国庆的烟花表演。</span><span>，</span><span>此际，</span><span>距离</span><span>17岁青少年</span><span>纳</span><span>赫尔</span><span>（</span><span>Nahel</span><span>）6月</span><span>27日</span><span>死亡后</span><span>，各地爆发</span><span>骚乱</span><span>打砸抢烧事件</span><span>已两周</span><span>的</span><span>时间。</span></span><span> </span></p><p </span></span><span><span>）</span></span><strong><span>。</span></strong><span> </span></p><p
