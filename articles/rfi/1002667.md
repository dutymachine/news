[乌克兰已申请加入《全面与进步跨太平洋伙伴关系协定》](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%BB%8F%E8%B4%B8/20230707-%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0%E5%B7%B2%E7%94%B3%E8%AF%B7%E5%8A%A0%E5%85%A5-%E5%85%A8%E9%9D%A2%E4%B8%8E%E8%BF%9B%E6%AD%A5%E8%B7%A8%E5%A4%AA%E5%B9%B3%E6%B4%8B%E4%BC%99%E4%BC%B4%E5%85%B3%E7%B3%BB%E5%8D%8F%E5%AE%9A)

<div>07/07/2023 - 13:17</div><img        </strong></p><div><p>新西兰外交部发言人说，申请过程的下一步将由CPTPP的所有成员决定，他们将于7月16日在该国城市奥克兰举行会议。</p><p><span><span><span><span>《全面与进步跨太平洋伙伴关系协定》成员包括澳大利亚、文莱、加拿大、智利、日本、马来西亚、墨西哥、新西兰、秘鲁、新加坡和越南，英国将成为第</span>12<span>个成员国。</span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>日本经济再生担当大臣后藤茂之在例行记者会上说，</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>已得知新西兰宣布已收到乌克兰加入</span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span>CPTPP的请求。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>后藤茂之</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span>表示，<span><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>日本作为</span></span></span></span></span><span><span><span><span><span>CPTPP成员，在市场准入和规则方面“必须仔细评估乌克兰是否完全达到该</span></span></span></span></span><span>协定</span><span><span><span><span><span>的高水平”。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><div
