[沙特与俄罗斯竞相减少原油供应以提振市场价格](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20230703-%E6%B2%99%E7%89%B9%E4%B8%8E%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF%E7%AB%9E%E7%9B%B8%E5%87%8F%E5%B0%91%E5%8E%9F%E6%B2%B9%E4%BE%9B%E5%BA%94%E4%BB%A5%E6%8F%90%E6%8C%AF%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC)
------

<div>03/07/2023 - 17:52</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/a66949d0-b432-11ed-8090-005056a90284/w:1280/p:16x9/2023-02-03T122344Z_1644104576_RC2ONU9F7F0R_RTRMADP_3_RUSSIA-BUDGET-OIL.JPG"><p><strong>继全球最大石油输出国沙特阿拉伯7月3日宣布延长石油减产措施之后，另一大石油输出国俄罗斯同日宣布每日减少原油出口50万桶。两项措施一经宣布，立即引发市场价格反弹。        </strong></p><div><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>沙特阿拉伯</span></span></span><span><span><span>3</span></span></span><span><span><span>日宣布在七、八两月维持将日产原油量降低</span></span></span><span><span><span>100</span></span></span><span><span><span>万桶的减产措施，以便支持出现下滑的市场价格。这意味着该国每日产油量将维持在</span></span></span><span><span><span>900</span></span></span><span><span><span>万桶左右。</span></span></span><span><span><span>沙特官方通讯社引述该国能源部消息称，延长减产措施意在强化欧佩克石油输出国组织及其盟国此前的稳定和平衡市场的决定。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>同一天，俄罗斯副总理也宣布，俄罗斯将在</span></span></span><span><span><span>8</span></span></span><span><span><span>月期间，将每日投放国际市场的原油总量减少</span></span></span><span><span><span>50</span></span></span><span><span><span>万桶，理由同样是平衡市场价格。不过，俄罗斯副总理没有明确俄罗斯是否相应减少产量。根据</span></span></span><span><span><span>Eikon</span></span></span><span><span><span>相关数据，俄罗斯日产原油大约在</span></span></span><span><span><span>911</span></span></span><span><span><span>万桶左右。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>沙特阿拉伯和俄罗斯降低市场供油的消息立即引发提振效应。</span></span></span><span><span><span>7</span></span></span><span><span><span>月</span></span></span><span><span><span>3</span></span></span><span><span><span>日，北海布伦特原油</span></span></span><span><span><span>9</span></span></span><span><span><span>月期货价格每桶上升</span></span></span><span><span><span>0.8</span></span></span><span><span><span>个百分点，国际标准时间</span></span></span><span><span><span>11</span></span></span><span><span><span>点时，报每桶</span></span></span><span><span><span>76.2</span></span></span><span><span><span>美元。同一时间，美国原油</span></span></span><span><span><span>8</span></span></span><span><span><span>月期货价格上扬</span></span></span><span><span><span>0.91</span></span></span><span><span><span>个百分点，报每桶</span></span></span><span><span><span>71.28</span></span></span><span><span><span>美元。但这种提振效应可以维持多久还是未知数。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>能源市场价格经历了</span></span></span><span><span><span>2021</span></span></span><span><span><span>年底开始的高峰之后，逐渐回落。价格起伏不定的同时，俄罗斯入侵乌克兰战争的冲击效应持续，中国疫情解封后的经济复苏势头疲软，这些因素都对能源市场需求产生影响。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>今年</span></span></span><span><span><span>4</span></span></span><span><span><span>月，包括沙特阿拉伯在内的多个产油国都在欧佩克组织及其盟国（OPEC+)架构下，宣布主动降低单日产量</span></span></span><span><span><span>100</span></span></span><span><span><span>万桶，以便支持油价。不过，这项措施并未能带来持久效应。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><p><span><span><span><span><span><span><span>沙特阿拉伯</span></span></span><span><span><span>5</span></span></span><span><span><span>月份开始实施的减产措施本应</span></span></span><span><span><span>7</span></span></span><span><span><span>月底到期。沙特官方通讯社</span></span></span><span><span><span>7</span></span></span><span><span><span>月</span></span></span><span><span><span>3</span></span></span><span><span><span>日宣布这项延长措施时还表示，减产措施有可能在</span></span></span><span><span><span>8</span></span></span><span><span><span>月之后再次延长。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p><div
