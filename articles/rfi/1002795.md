[台湾今举实弹演习](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20230703-%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BB%8A%E4%B8%BE%E5%AE%9E%E5%BC%B9%E6%BC%94%E4%B9%A0)
------

<div>03/07/2023 - 12:12</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/2d47cf24-da55-11ed-84bd-005056bfb2b6/w:1280/p:16x9/2023-04-11T220836Z_1419833292_RC2LC0ACYU9J_RTRMADP_3_CHINA-TAIWAN.JPG"><p><strong>台湾将于周一启动为期两天的军事演习，包括导弹实弹发射，这是台湾面对中国对台湾的自治权日益增长的威胁而进行的最大年度演习的一部分。        </strong></p><div><p>据法新社今天报道称，台湾官方称，周一和周二的实弹演习是为了应对中国在台湾海峡日益增长的军事存在，台湾国防部几乎每天都报告有中国军舰和飞机扰行。</p><p>6月底，8架中国军机接近台湾控制的水域，距离台湾海岸44公里。21日，山东号航空母舰在其他船只的陪护下穿越海峡。4月，北京在台湾周围进行了为期三天的包围演习。该报道称，这是为了报复台湾总统蔡英文和美国众议院议长凯文-麦卡锡之间的会晤。</p><p>中国反对台北与外国之间的任何官方接触。</p><p>台湾此次实弹演习将在台湾最南部的屏东县举行。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gwT.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25BA%259A%25E6%25B4%25B2%2F20230703-%25E5%258F%25B0%25E6%25B9%25BE%25E4%25BB%258A%25E4%25B8%25BE%25E5%25AE%259E%25E5%25BC%25B9%25E6%25BC%2594%25E4%25B9%25A0&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BB%8A%E4%B8%BE%E5%AE%9E%E5%BC%B9%E6%BC%94%E4%B9%A0%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gwT.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BB%8A%E4%B8%BE%E5%AE%9E%E5%BC%B9%E6%BC%94%E4%B9%A0%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gwT.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gwT.T&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E4%BB%8A%E4%B8%BE%E5%AE%9E%E5%BC%B9%E6%BC%94%E4%B9%A0&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
