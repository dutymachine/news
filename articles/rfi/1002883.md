[国产C919客机突然停飞](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230630-%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7c919%E5%AE%A2%E6%9C%BA%E7%AA%81%E7%84%B6%E5%81%9C%E9%A3%9E)
------

<div>30/06/2023 - 22:50</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/c0439eba-1787-11ee-a597-005056a90321/w:1280/p:16x9/premier-vol-d-essai-pour-le-troisieme-prototype-du-c919-chinois.png"><p><strong></strong></p><div><p>这架上海东⽅航空公司唯一的一架C919型客机，29日执行编号MU9197的航班，上午11点06分抵达成都天府机场后，按计画是要接着执行编号MU9198的返程航班，在中午12点半起飞返回上海虹桥。然而，返程航班延误到了下午2点16分才起飞，而且执飞的客机不是C919，而是更换成了一架空客A320型客机。</p><p>作为被官方标榜为“民族骄傲”的首架国产大型客机，C919今年5⽉28⽇开始投入运营。每天执行从上海虹桥往返成都天府机场的飞行任务。然而在飞行才刚满⼀个⽉的时候却发⽣了航班延误与更换航机的状况。</p><p>根据民航信息网站“飞行雷达24（flightradar24）”所提供的资料，30日依然是由空客A320执行原来由C919飞行的航班。</p><p> </p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gSV.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25B8%25AD%25E5%259B%25BD%2F20230630-%25E5%259B%25BD%25E4%25BA%25A7c919%25E5%25AE%25A2%25E6%259C%25BA%25E7%25AA%2581%25E7%2584%25B6%25E5%2581%259C%25E9%25A3%259E&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7C919%E5%AE%A2%E6%9C%BA%E7%AA%81%E7%84%B6%E5%81%9C%E9%A3%9E%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gSV.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7C919%E5%AE%A2%E6%9C%BA%E7%AA%81%E7%84%B6%E5%81%9C%E9%A3%9E%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gSV.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9gSV.T&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%A7C919%E5%AE%A2%E6%9C%BA%E7%AA%81%E7%84%B6%E5%81%9C%E9%A3%9E&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
