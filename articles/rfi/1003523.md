[美国将允许韩国和台湾芯片制造商在华保留业务](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20230612-%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%86%E5%85%81%E8%AE%B8%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E5%92%8C%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E8%8A%AF%E7%89%87%E5%88%B6%E9%80%A0%E5%95%86%E4%BF%9D%E7%95%99%E5%9C%A8%E5%8D%8E%E4%B8%9A%E5%8A%A1)
