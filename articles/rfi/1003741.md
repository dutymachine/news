[淀粉柠檬酸充神药蒙坑不少中国人](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230605-%E6%B7%80%E7%B2%89%E6%9F%A0%E6%AA%AC%E9%85%B8%E5%85%85%E7%A5%9E%E8%8D%AF%E8%92%99%E5%9D%91%E4%B8%8D%E5%B0%91%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA)
