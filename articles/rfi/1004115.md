[韩国否认准备向乌克兰援供炮弹](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20230525-%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E5%90%A6%E8%AE%A4%E5%87%86%E5%A4%87%E5%90%91%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0%E6%8F%B4%E4%BE%9B%E7%82%AE%E5%BC%B9)
