[法国准备对乌克兰提供安全保证，防止未来可能的侵略](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20230523-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E5%87%86%E5%A4%87%E5%AF%B9%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0%E6%8F%90%E4%BE%9B%E5%AE%89%E5%85%A8%E4%BF%9D%E8%AF%81%EF%BC%8C%E9%98%B2%E6%AD%A2%E6%9C%AA%E6%9D%A5%E5%8F%AF%E8%83%BD%E7%9A%84%E4%BE%B5%E7%95%A5)
