[伊朗田径协会主席因女运动员未戴头巾参赛而下台](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20230507-%E4%BC%8A%E6%9C%97%E7%94%B0%E5%BE%84%E5%8D%8F%E4%BC%9A%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E5%9B%A0%E5%A5%B3%E8%BF%90%E5%8A%A8%E5%91%98%E6%9C%AA%E6%88%B4%E5%A4%B4%E5%B7%BE%E5%8F%82%E8%B5%9B%E8%80%8C%E4%B8%8B%E5%8F%B0)
