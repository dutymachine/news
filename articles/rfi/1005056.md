[法国开发署2022预算重视非洲](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230428-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E5%BC%80%E5%8F%91%E7%BD%B22022%E9%A2%84%E7%AE%97%E9%87%8D%E8%A7%86%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2)
