[中国宣布将派特别代表前往乌克兰访问](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230426-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%A3%E5%B8%83%E5%B0%86%E6%B4%BE%E7%89%B9%E5%88%AB%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E5%89%8D%E5%BE%80%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0%E8%AE%BF%E9%97%AE)
