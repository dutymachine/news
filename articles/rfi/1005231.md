[南苏丹对苏丹局势越来越不安](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230423-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E5%8D%97%E8%8B%8F%E4%B8%B9%E5%AF%B9%E8%8B%8F%E4%B8%B9%E5%B1%80%E5%8A%BF%E8%B6%8A%E6%9D%A5%E8%B6%8A%E4%B8%8D%E5%AE%89)
