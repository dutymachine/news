[英国电信去年曾举行兵棋推演，测试台海有事时供应链风险](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%BB%8F%E8%B4%B8/20230416-%E8%8B%B1%E5%9B%BD%E7%94%B5%E4%BF%A1%E4%B8%BE%E8%A1%8C%E5%85%B5%E6%A3%8B%E6%8E%A8%E6%BC%94%EF%BC%8C%E6%B5%8B%E8%AF%95%E5%8F%B0%E6%B5%B7%E6%9C%89%E4%BA%8B%E6%97%B6%E7%9A%84%E4%BE%9B%E5%BA%94%E9%93%BE%E9%A3%8E%E9%99%A9)
