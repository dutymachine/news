[俄方正式起诉美国记者格什科维奇从事间谍活动](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20230407-%E4%BF%84%E6%96%B9%E6%AD%A3%E5%BC%8F%E8%B5%B7%E8%AF%89%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E8%AE%B0%E8%80%85%E6%A0%BC%E4%BB%80%E7%A7%91%E7%BB%B4%E5%A5%87%E4%BB%8E%E4%BA%8B%E9%97%B4%E8%B0%8D%E6%B4%BB%E5%8A%A8)
