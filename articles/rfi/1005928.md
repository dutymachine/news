[堆在港口的西非棉花期待买家](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230401-%E5%A0%86%E5%9C%A8%E6%B8%AF%E5%8F%A3%E7%9A%84%E8%A5%BF%E9%9D%9E%E6%A3%89%E8%8A%B1%E6%9C%9F%E5%BE%85%E4%B9%B0%E5%AE%B6)
