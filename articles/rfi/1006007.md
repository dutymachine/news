[白宫：俄对被捕记者间谍罪的指控是“荒谬的”](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%BE%8E%E6%B4%B2/20230330-%E7%99%BD%E5%AE%AB-%E4%BF%84%E5%AF%B9%E8%A2%AB%E6%8D%95%E8%AE%B0%E8%80%85%E9%97%B4%E8%B0%8D%E7%BD%AA%E7%9A%84%E6%8C%87%E6%8E%A7%E6%98%AF-%E8%8D%92%E8%B0%AC%E7%9A%84)
