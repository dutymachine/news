[佩斯科夫：普习非正式会谈时长出人意料，体现双方互信精神](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20230326-%E4%BD%A9%E6%96%AF%E7%A7%91%E5%A4%AB-%E6%99%AE%E4%B9%A0%E9%9D%9E%E6%AD%A3%E5%BC%8F%E4%BC%9A%E8%B0%88%E6%97%B6%E9%95%BF%E5%87%BA%E4%BA%BA%E6%84%8F%E6%96%99%EF%BC%8C%E4%BD%93%E7%8E%B0%E5%8F%8C%E6%96%B9%E4%BA%92%E4%BF%A1%E7%B2%BE%E7%A5%9E)
