[乌击剑联合会对俄与白俄选手重返赛场表示愤慨](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20230310-%E4%B9%8C%E5%87%BB%E5%89%91%E8%81%94%E5%90%88%E4%BC%9A%E5%AF%B9%E4%BF%84%E4%B8%8E%E7%99%BD%E4%BF%84%E9%80%89%E6%89%8B%E9%87%8D%E8%BF%94%E8%B5%9B%E5%9C%BA%E8%A1%A8%E7%A4%BA%E6%84%A4%E6%85%A8)
