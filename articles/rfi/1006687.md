[巴黎圣母院重建工程：明年年底重新对公众开放](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/20230310-%E5%B7%B4%E9%BB%8E%E5%9C%A3%E6%AF%8D%E9%99%A2%E9%87%8D%E5%BB%BA%E5%B7%A5%E7%A8%8B-%E6%98%8E%E5%B9%B4%E5%B9%B4%E5%BA%95%E9%87%8D%E6%96%B0%E5%AF%B9%E5%85%AC%E4%BC%97%E5%BC%80%E6%94%BE)
