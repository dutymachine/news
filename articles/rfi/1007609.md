[芝麻:亚洲需求带动非洲价格](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230209-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E7%BB%8F%E8%B4%B8-%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E8%8A%9D%E9%BA%BB%E9%9C%80%E6%B1%82%E5%B8%A6%E5%8A%A8%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2%E4%BB%B7%E6%A0%BC)
