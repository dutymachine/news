[科特迪瓦有意升温与马里关系](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20230112-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E7%A7%91%E7%89%B9%E8%BF%AA%E7%93%A6%E6%9C%89%E6%84%8F%E5%8D%87%E6%B8%A9%E4%B8%8E%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C%E5%85%B3%E7%B3%BB)
