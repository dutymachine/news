[投资人要嘉能可为碳排放承责](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%94%9F%E6%80%81/20230108-%E6%8A%95%E8%B5%84%E4%BA%BA%E8%A6%81%E5%98%89%E8%83%BD%E5%8F%AF%E4%B8%BA%E7%A2%B3%E6%8E%92%E6%94%BE%E6%89%BF%E8%B4%A3)
