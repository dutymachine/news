[法国欢迎世界气候峰会进展 但对缺乏雄心壮志遗憾](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/20221120-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E6%AC%A2%E8%BF%8E%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E6%B0%94%E5%80%99%E5%B3%B0%E4%BC%9A%E8%BF%9B%E5%B1%95-%E4%BD%86%E5%AF%B9%E7%BC%BA%E4%B9%8F%E9%9B%84%E5%BF%83%E5%A3%AE%E5%BF%97%E9%81%97%E6%86%BE)
