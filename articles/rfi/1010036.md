[英媒曝普京在非洲藏金与现钞总值123亿镑](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20221119-%E8%8B%B1%E5%AA%92%E6%9B%9D%E6%99%AE%E4%BA%AC%E5%9C%A8%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2%E8%97%8F%E9%87%91%E4%B8%8E%E7%8E%B0%E9%92%9E%E6%80%BB%E5%80%BC123%E4%BA%BF%E9%95%91)
