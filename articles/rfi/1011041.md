[白宫：拜登无意在G20和沙特王储会面](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20221016-%E7%99%BD%E5%AE%AB-%E6%8B%9C%E7%99%BB%E6%97%A0%E6%84%8F%E5%9C%A8g20%E5%92%8C%E6%B2%99%E7%89%B9%E7%8E%8B%E5%82%A8%E4%BC%9A%E9%9D%A2)
