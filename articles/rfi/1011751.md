[大陆赠台熊猫团团严重脑病变卧龙基地不排除赴台了解](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20220924-%E5%A4%A7%E9%99%86%E8%B5%A0%E5%8F%B0%E7%86%8A%E7%8C%AB%E5%9B%A2%E5%9B%A2%E4%B8%A5%E9%87%8D%E8%84%91%E7%97%85%E5%8F%98%E5%8D%A7%E9%BE%99%E5%9F%BA%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8D%E6%8E%92%E9%99%A4%E8%B5%B4%E5%8F%B0%E4%BA%86%E8%A7%A3)
