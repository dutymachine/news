[美国与韩国对朝鲜拒绝经援换无核表遗憾](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20220819-%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E4%B8%8E%E9%9F%A9%E5%9B%BD%E5%AF%B9%E6%9C%9D%E9%B2%9C%E6%8B%92%E7%BB%9D%E7%BB%8F%E6%8F%B4%E6%8D%A2%E6%97%A0%E6%A0%B8%E8%A1%A8%E9%81%97%E6%86%BE)
