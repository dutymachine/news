[布基纳法索:有女性涉恐怖主义](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20220730-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-nicolas-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E5%B8%83%E5%9F%BA%E7%BA%B3%E6%B3%95%E7%B4%A2-%E6%9C%89%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7%E6%B6%89%E6%81%90%E6%80%96%E4%B8%BB%E4%B9%89)
