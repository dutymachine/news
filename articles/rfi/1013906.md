[中国更多城市封锁清零](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20220716-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E6%9B%B4%E5%A4%9A%E5%9F%8E%E5%B8%82%E5%B0%81%E9%94%81%E6%B8%85%E9%9B%B6)
