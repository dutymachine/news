[方济各任命3名女性参与梵蒂冈主教提名审查](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E5%9B%BD%E9%99%85/20220713-%E6%96%B9%E6%B5%8E%E5%90%84%E4%BB%BB%E5%91%BD3%E5%90%8D%E5%A5%B3%E6%80%A7%E5%8F%82%E4%B8%8E%E6%A2%B5%E8%92%82%E5%86%88%E4%B8%BB%E6%95%99%E6%8F%90%E5%90%8D%E5%AE%A1%E6%9F%A5)
