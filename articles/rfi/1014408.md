[马里:中俄弃权促Minusma延长](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20220701-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-nicolas-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E5%9C%B0%E7%BC%98%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB-%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C-%E4%B8%AD%E4%BF%84%E5%BC%83%E6%9D%83%E4%BF%83minusma%E5%BB%B6%E9%95%BF)
