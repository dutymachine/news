[埃尔多安与瑞典及芬兰领导人通电话就两国加入北约交换意见](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20220521-%E5%9F%83%E5%B0%94%E5%A4%9A%E5%AE%89%E4%B8%8E%E7%91%9E%E5%85%B8%E5%8F%8A%E8%8A%AC%E5%85%B0%E9%A2%86%E5%AF%BC%E4%BA%BA%E9%80%9A%E7%94%B5%E8%AF%9D%E5%B0%B1%E4%B8%A4%E5%9B%BD%E5%8A%A0%E5%85%A5%E5%8C%97%E7%BA%A6%E4%BA%A4%E6%8D%A2%E6%84%8F%E8%A7%81)
