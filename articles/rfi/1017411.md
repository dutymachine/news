[俄罗斯携核弹头轰炸机侵入瑞典领空 传美国核轰炸机以眼还眼即飞俄边境](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20220401-%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF%E6%90%BA%E6%A0%B8%E5%BC%B9%E5%A4%B4%E8%BD%B0%E7%82%B8%E6%9C%BA%E4%BE%B5%E5%85%A5%E7%91%9E%E5%85%B8%E9%A2%86%E7%A9%BA-%E4%BC%A0%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E6%A0%B8%E8%BD%B0%E7%82%B8%E6%9C%BA%E4%BB%A5%E7%9C%BC%E8%BF%98%E7%9C%BC%E5%8D%B3%E9%A3%9E%E4%BF%84%E8%BE%B9%E5%A2%83)
