[马里:西非经共体特使启动协商](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A/20220226-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-cedeao-%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C-%E8%A5%BF%E9%9D%9E%E7%BB%8F%E5%85%B1%E4%BD%93%E7%89%B9%E4%BD%BF%E5%90%AF%E5%8A%A8%E5%8D%8F%E5%95%86)
