[苏丹:115名人权捍卫者获释](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A/20220225-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-onu-%E8%8B%8F%E4%B8%B9-115%E5%90%8D%E4%BA%BA%E6%9D%83%E6%8D%8D%E5%8D%AB%E8%80%85%E8%8E%B7%E9%87%8A)
