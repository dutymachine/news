[索马里兰派多名部长出访台湾](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20220213-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-geopolitics-%E7%B4%A2%E9%A9%AC%E9%87%8C%E5%85%B0%E6%B4%BE%E5%A4%9A%E5%90%8D%E9%83%A8%E9%95%BF%E5%87%BA%E8%AE%BF%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE)
