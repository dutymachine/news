[刚果“民”一家金矿5名中国人今凌晨遭绑架](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20211121-%E5%88%9A%E6%9E%9C-%E6%B0%91-%E4%B8%80%E5%AE%B6%E9%87%91%E7%9F%BF5%E5%90%8D%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E4%BB%8A%E5%87%8C%E6%99%A8%E9%81%AD%E7%BB%91%E6%9E%B6)
