[美国将中俄等10国列入压制宗教自由“特别关注国”黑名单](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20211117-%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD%E5%B0%86%E4%B8%AD%E4%BF%84%E7%AD%8910%E5%9B%BD%E5%88%97%E5%85%A5-%E4%B8%A5%E9%87%8D%E4%BE%B5%E7%8A%AF%E5%AE%97%E6%95%99%E8%87%AA%E7%94%B1-%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E9%BB%91%E5%90%8D%E5%8D%95)
