[乌克兰否认 俄在两国边界附近集结军队](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20211101-%E4%B9%8C%E5%85%8B%E5%85%B0%E5%90%A6%E8%AE%A4-%E4%BF%84%E5%9C%A8%E4%B8%A4%E5%9B%BD%E8%BE%B9%E7%95%8C%E9%99%84%E8%BF%91%E9%9B%86%E7%BB%93%E5%86%9B%E9%98%9F)
