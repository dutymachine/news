[法国人道者尼日尔遇害 11名嫌犯被捕](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/20211023-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-france-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E4%BA%BA%E9%81%93%E8%80%85%E5%B0%BC%E6%97%A5%E5%B0%94%E9%81%87%E5%AE%B3-11%E5%90%8D%E5%AB%8C%E7%8A%AF%E8%A2%AB%E6%8D%95)
