[乍得:两支对话代表团分赴法国埃及](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20211020-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E5%9C%B0%E7%BC%98%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB-%E4%B9%8D%E5%BE%97-%E4%B8%A4%E6%94%AF%E5%AF%B9%E8%AF%9D%E4%BB%A3%E8%A1%A8%E5%9B%A2%E5%88%86%E8%B5%B4%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E5%9F%83%E5%8F%8A)
