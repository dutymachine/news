[乍得:过渡委员会主席任命临时议会](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20210925-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-politics-%E4%B9%8D%E5%BE%97-%E8%BF%87%E6%B8%A1%E5%A7%94%E5%91%98%E4%BC%9A%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E4%BB%BB%E5%91%BD%E4%B8%B4%E6%97%B6%E8%AE%AE%E4%BC%9A)
