[南苏丹7300多万美元公款遭挪用](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20210924-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-finance-%E5%8D%97%E8%8B%8F%E4%B8%B97300%E5%A4%9A%E4%B8%87%E7%BE%8E%E5%85%83%E5%85%AC%E6%AC%BE%E9%81%AD%E6%8C%AA%E7%94%A8)
