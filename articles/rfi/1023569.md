[乍得首都建造环城防御战壕引发担忧](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%A4%BE%E4%BC%9A/20210924-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-geopolitics-%E4%B9%8D%E5%BE%97%E9%A6%96%E9%83%BD%E5%BB%BA%E9%80%A0%E7%8E%AF%E5%9F%8E%E9%98%B2%E5%BE%A1%E6%88%98%E5%A3%95%E5%BC%95%E5%8F%91%E6%8B%85%E5%BF%A7)
