[支联会元老李卓人和何俊仁狱中吁会员支持解散](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20210921-%E6%94%AF%E8%81%94%E4%BC%9A%E5%85%83%E8%80%81%E6%9D%8E%E5%8D%93%E4%BA%BA%E5%92%8C%E4%BD%95%E4%BF%8A%E4%BB%81%E7%8B%B1%E4%B8%AD%E5%90%81%E4%BC%9A%E5%91%98%E6%94%AF%E6%8C%81%E8%A7%A3%E6%95%A3)
