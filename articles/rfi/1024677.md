[刚果(金)和赞比亚放下争端勘定边界](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20210821-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-g%C3%A9opolitique-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E5%88%9A%E6%9E%9C-%E9%87%91-%E5%92%8C%E8%B5%9E%E6%AF%94%E4%BA%9A%E6%94%BE%E4%B8%8B%E4%BA%89%E7%AB%AF%E5%8B%98%E5%AE%9A%E8%BE%B9%E7%95%8C)
