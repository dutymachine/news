[法国暂缓与埃塞俄比亚军事合作协议](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB/20210814-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-france-%E5%9C%B0%E7%BC%98%E6%94%BF%E6%B2%BB-%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD%E6%9A%82%E7%BC%93%E4%B8%8E%E5%9F%83%E5%A1%9E%E4%BF%84%E6%AF%94%E4%BA%9A%E5%86%9B%E4%BA%8B%E5%90%88%E4%BD%9C%E5%8D%8F%E8%AE%AE)
