[戛纳电影节独树一帜设立环境主题特别展映单元](http://www.rfi.fr//cn/%E6%B3%95%E5%9B%BD/20210713-%E6%88%9B%E7%BA%B3%E7%94%B5%E5%BD%B1%E8%8A%82%E7%8B%AC%E6%A0%91%E4%B8%80%E5%B8%9C%E8%AE%BE%E7%AB%8B%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83%E4%B8%BB%E9%A2%98%E7%89%B9%E5%88%AB%E5%B1%95%E6%98%A0%E5%8D%95%E5%85%83)
