[日本札幌放弃申办2030年冬奥会](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20231011-%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%9C%AD%E5%B9%8C%E6%94%BE%E5%BC%83%E7%94%B3%E5%8A%9E2030%E5%B9%B4%E5%86%AC%E5%A5%A5%E4%BC%9A)
------

<div>11/10/2023 - 15:42</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/fe02fc46-162d-11ea-a189-005056bf7c53/w:1280/p:16x9/fde0003d8cde826302738c2ba1110f6b769d72fb.jpg"><p><strong>据日本官员今天报告说，札幌市已放弃申办 2030 年冬奥会和残奥会，将目标改为申办 2034 年或更晚的冬奥会。        </strong></p><div><p>据日本读卖新闻今天报道称，札幌市市长秋元胜宏和日本奥委会主席山下康弘在东京会谈后的新闻发布会上宣布了这一决定。</p><p>札幌市政府一直在努力申请主办 2030 年奥运会，但由于与 2021 年举办的东京奥运会和残奥会相关的腐败等丑闻而引发公众不信任，最终得出结论，该市政府将无法获得居民的支持。</p><p>如果札幌市将申办时间改为 2034 年或更晚举办的冬奥会，可能获得更好的支持。</p><p>日本奥委会称已决定支持该市的决定。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0fC.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25BA%259A%25E6%25B4%25B2%2F20231011-%25E6%2597%25A5%25E6%259C%25AC%25E6%259C%25AD%25E5%25B9%258C%25E6%2594%25BE%25E5%25BC%2583%25E7%2594%25B3%25E5%258A%259E2030%25E5%25B9%25B4%25E5%2586%25AC%25E5%25A5%25A5%25E4%25BC%259A&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%9C%AD%E5%B9%8C%E6%94%BE%E5%BC%83%E7%94%B3%E5%8A%9E2030%E5%B9%B4%E5%86%AC%E5%A5%A5%E4%BC%9A%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0fC.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%9C%AD%E5%B9%8C%E6%94%BE%E5%BC%83%E7%94%B3%E5%8A%9E2030%E5%B9%B4%E5%86%AC%E5%A5%A5%E4%BC%9A%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0fC.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://x.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0fC.X&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%9C%AD%E5%B9%8C%E6%94%BE%E5%BC%83%E7%94%B3%E5%8A%9E2030%E5%B9%B4%E5%86%AC%E5%A5%A5%E4%BC%9A&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
