[拜登：加沙应与约旦河西岸统一在巴权力机构下](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E4%B8%9C/20231118-%E6%8B%9C%E7%99%BB-%E5%8A%A0%E6%B2%99%E5%BA%94%E4%B8%8E%E7%BA%A6%E6%97%A6%E6%B2%B3%E8%A5%BF%E5%B2%B8%E7%BB%9F%E4%B8%80%E5%9C%A8%E5%B7%B4%E6%9D%83%E5%8A%9B%E6%9C%BA%E6%9E%84%E4%B8%8B)

<div>18/11/2023 - 22:09</div><img
