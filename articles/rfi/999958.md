[Monusco预2024撤出刚果(金)](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20240115-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-monusco%E9%A2%842024%E6%92%A4%E5%87%BA%E5%88%9A%E6%9E%9C-%E9%87%91)
