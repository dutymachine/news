[中国铜业大亨惊传失联](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20231010-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E9%93%9C%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E4%BA%A8%E6%83%8A%E4%BC%A0%E5%A4%B1%E8%81%94)
------

<div>10/10/2023 - 15:22</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/fd604a04-676f-11ee-a5e7-005056bf30b7/w:1280/p:16x9/MKTes.gif"><p><strong>中国最大的铜贸易商之一“迈科金属”（Maike Metals）自去年以来就陷入财务流动性危机，交易活动传出已基本停止，最新消息指出，知情人士透露，迈科创始人何金碧，正处于失联状态，据信何金碧已经被中国警方拘留，并在他的家乡陕西省接受审问，但知情人士没有说明何金碧被拘留的原因。        </strong></p><div><p>据自由时报今天引述彭博社消息，中国铜业大亨惊传失联，据信被拘留审问。</p><p>《彭博》今天称多次尝试透过电话、短信联系何金碧和其家人，但均未成功；迈科拒绝在电子邮件中对此事发表评论；陕西省公安没有回复要求评论的传真。</p><p>迈科之前经手了中国铜进口量的4分之1，因此该集团垮台，对行业影响重大。迈科今年2月向法院提出“初步重组”请求，寻求重新开始金属交易。</p><p>迈科今年在香港被荷兰国际集团（ING Groep）起诉，IGG指控迈科没有偿还1.47亿美元的债务。根据法庭文件，该案涉及迈科1家贸易部门所欠的逾期付款。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0T7.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25B8%25AD%25E5%259B%25BD%2F20231010-%25E4%25B8%25AD%25E5%259B%25BD%25E9%2593%259C%25E4%25B8%259A%25E5%25A4%25A7%25E4%25BA%25A8%25E6%2583%258A%25E4%25BC%25A0%25E5%25A4%25B1%25E8%2581%2594&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E9%93%9C%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E4%BA%A8%E6%83%8A%E4%BC%A0%E5%A4%B1%E8%81%94%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0T7.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E9%93%9C%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E4%BA%A8%E6%83%8A%E4%BC%A0%E5%A4%B1%E8%81%94%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0T7.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://x.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2FA0T7.X&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E9%93%9C%E4%B8%9A%E5%A4%A7%E4%BA%A8%E6%83%8A%E4%BC%A0%E5%A4%B1%E8%81%94&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
