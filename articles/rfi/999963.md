[福岛第一核电站5吨半含放射性的水泄漏](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20240207-%E7%A6%8F%E5%B2%9B%E7%AC%AC%E4%B8%80%E6%A0%B8%E7%94%B5%E7%AB%995%E5%90%A8%E5%8D%8A%E5%90%AB%E6%94%BE%E5%B0%84%E6%80%A7%E7%9A%84%E6%B0%B4%E6%B3%84%E6%BC%8F)
