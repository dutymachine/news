[刚果(金)霍乱病例显著增多](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20231106-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E5%88%9A%E6%9E%9C-%E9%87%91-%E9%9C%8D%E4%B9%B1%E7%97%85%E4%BE%8B%E6%98%BE%E8%91%97%E5%A2%9E%E5%A4%9A)
