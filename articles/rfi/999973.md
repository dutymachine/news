[南海紧张之际 菲律宾总统将访日](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2/20231211-%E5%8D%97%E6%B5%B7%E7%B4%A7%E5%BC%A0%E4%B9%8B%E9%99%85-%E8%8F%B2%E5%BE%8B%E5%AE%BE%E6%80%BB%E7%BB%9F%E5%B0%86%E8%AE%BF%E6%97%A5)

<div>11/12/2023 - 11:33</div><img
