[塞内加尔：官方候选人承认败选](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20240325-%E5%A1%9E%E5%86%85%E5%8A%A0%E5%B0%94-%E5%AE%98%E6%96%B9%E5%80%99%E9%80%89%E4%BA%BA%E6%89%BF%E8%AE%A4%E8%B4%A5%E9%80%89)
