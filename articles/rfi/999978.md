[肯尼亚悼空难殉职的军方首长](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2/20240419-rfi-%E6%B3%95%E5%B9%BF-%E5%B0%BC%E5%8F%A4%E6%8B%89-%E9%9D%9E%E6%B4%B2-%E8%82%AF%E5%B0%BC%E4%BA%9A%E6%82%BC%E7%A9%BA%E9%9A%BE%E6%AE%89%E8%81%8C%E7%9A%84%E5%86%9B%E6%96%B9%E9%A6%96%E9%95%BF)
