[乌方称“被炸死”的黑海舰队司令在俄电视中现身](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E6%AC%A7%E6%B4%B2/20230926-%E4%B9%8C%E6%96%B9%E7%A7%B0-%E8%A2%AB%E7%82%B8%E6%AD%BB-%E7%9A%84%E9%BB%91%E6%B5%B7%E8%88%B0%E9%98%9F%E5%8F%B8%E4%BB%A4%E5%9C%A8%E4%BF%84%E7%94%B5%E8%A7%86%E4%B8%AD%E7%8E%B0%E8%BA%AB)
