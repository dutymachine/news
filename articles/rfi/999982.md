[亚太经合峰会未掩对乌战分歧](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E7%BE%8E%E5%9B%BD/20231118-%E4%BA%9A%E5%A4%AA%E7%BB%8F%E5%90%88%E5%B3%B0%E4%BC%9A%E6%9C%AA%E6%8E%A9%E5%AF%B9%E4%B9%8C%E6%88%98%E5%88%86%E6%AD%A7)
