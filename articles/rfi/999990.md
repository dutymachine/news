[中国宣布习近平参加亚太经合峰会 确认见拜登](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20231111-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%A3%E5%B8%83%E4%B9%A0%E8%BF%91%E5%B9%B3%E5%8F%82%E5%8A%A0%E4%BA%9A%E5%A4%AA%E7%BB%8F%E5%90%88%E5%B3%B0%E4%BC%9A-%E7%A1%AE%E8%AE%A4%E8%A7%81%E6%8B%9C%E7%99%BB)

<div>11/11/2023 - 09:59</div><img
