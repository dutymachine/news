[中国足协前主席被控受贿](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20230926-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E8%B6%B3%E5%8D%8F%E5%89%8D%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7%E5%8F%97%E8%B4%BF)
------

<div>26/09/2023 - 13:57</div><img src="https://s.rfi.fr/media/display/287c0cf8-ce7a-11ed-a14f-005056bf30b7/w:1280/p:16x9/iStock-1030811736.jpg"><p><strong>中国国家电视台中央电视台今天报告，中国当局指控国家足协前主席陈戌源受贿。中国当局在体育包括足协反腐败调查持续未歇。中国足协多位高级官员均已落马。        </strong></p><div><p>中国电视公开报道中国足协前主席被控受贿。湖北省当局指控中国足协前主席陈戌源利用职务和职权，“为他人谋取利益”、“非法收受他人财物”。</p><p>中央电视台新闻说，陈戌源的案件现已提交检察官审理。</p><p>陈戌源还曾担任足协党组副书记。</p><p>8月，中国官方媒体报道称，足球高级官员、中超联赛主席刘军已被当局带走接受调查。据体育监管机构发布的声明，今年一月，两名前中国足协官员因涉嫌“严重违法”而接受调查。</p><p>三月份，另外两名足球高级官员因涉嫌严重违法而受到调查。</p><p>消息均没有足球官员腐败的细节内容。</p><div data-selfpromo-newsletter></div><div data-selfpromo-app></div></div><div><div>分享 :</div><div><a href="https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=113191652055439&amp;href=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9xx6.F&amp;redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.rfi.fr%2Fcn%2F%25E4%25B8%25AD%25E5%259B%25BD%2F20230926-%25E4%25B8%25AD%25E5%259B%25BD%25E8%25B6%25B3%25E5%258D%258F%25E5%2589%258D%25E4%25B8%25BB%25E5%25B8%25AD%25E8%25A2%25AB%25E6%258E%25A7%25E5%258F%2597%25E8%25B4%25BF&amp;locale=zh_CN" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="whatsapp://send?text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E8%B6%B3%E5%8D%8F%E5%89%8D%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7%E5%8F%97%E8%B4%BF%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9xx6.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://web.whatsapp.com/send?text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E8%B6%B3%E5%8D%8F%E5%89%8D%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7%E5%8F%97%E8%B4%BF%20-%20https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9xx6.W" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><a href="https://x.com/intent/tweet?url=https%3A%2F%2Frfi.my%2F9xx6.X&amp;via=RFI_Cn&amp;related=RFI_Cn&amp;text=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E8%B6%B3%E5%8D%8F%E5%89%8D%E4%B8%BB%E5%B8%AD%E8%A2%AB%E6%8E%A7%E5%8F%97%E8%B4%BF&amp;lang=zh-cn" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow"><span></span></a><span data-root-share><share-button v-on:open="openModal"></share-button><share-modal v-if="displayModal" v-on:close="closeModal"></share-modal></span></div></div>
