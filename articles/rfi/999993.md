[中国经济节后仍不振连带亚洲股市暴跌](https://www.rfi.fr/cn/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD/20240219-%E4%B8%AD%E5%9B%BD%E7%BB%8F%E6%B5%8E%E8%8A%82%E5%90%8E%E4%BB%8D%E4%B8%8D%E6%8C%AF%E8%BF%9E%E5%B8%A6%E4%BA%9A%E6%B4%B2%E8%82%A1%E5%B8%82%E6%9A%B4%E8%B7%8C)
------

<div>19/02/2024 - 10:52</div><img
