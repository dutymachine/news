[菲律宾谴责中国在南中国海设立“浮动障碍”](https://www.voachinese.com/a/philippines-condemns-chinese-floating-barrier-in-south-china-sea-20230924/7282007.html)
------

<div><i>Sun, 24 Sep 2023 23:19:42 GMT</i></div><img src="https://images.weserv.nl?url=gdb.voanews.com/01000000-0a00-0242-b5cb-08dbbcc63a92_r1_s_w900.jpg" width="100%"><div><small style="color: #999;">2023年9月22日拍摄的照片显示，菲律宾渔业和水生资源局的一艘船只在有争议的南中国海中国控制的斯卡伯勒浅滩即中方所称黄岩岛入口附近航行时，中国海警船只拦截了该船。 （法新社图片）</small></div><p>MANILA, Sept 24 (Reuters) - The Philippines on Sunday accused China's coast guard of installing a "floating barrier" in a disputed area of the South China Sea, saying it prevented Filipinos from entering and fishing in the area.<br />菲律宾周日(9月24日)指责中国海警在南中国海争议海域设置“浮动障碍”，阻止菲律宾人进入该地区捕鱼。<br /><br />Manila's coast guard and Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources "strongly condemn" China's installation of the barrier in part of the Scarborough Shoal, Commodore Jay Tarriela, a coast guard spokesperson, posted on the X social media platform, formerly Twitter.<br />马尼拉海岸警卫队发言人杰伊·塔里埃拉(Jay Tarriela)准将在前身为推特的社交媒体平台X上发帖称，马尼拉海岸警卫队和渔业及水生资源局“强烈谴责”中国在斯卡伯勒浅滩(中国称黄岩岛)部分地区安装障碍。<br /><br />Advertisement · Scroll to continue<br /><br />The barrier blocking fishermen from the shoal was depriving them of their fishing and livelihood activities", he said.<br />他说：“阻挡渔民进入该浅滩的屏障剥夺了他们的捕鱼和生计活动。”<br /><br />"The (Philippine Coast Guard) will continue to work closely with all concerned government agencies to address these challenges, uphold our maritime rights and protect our maritime domains," Tarriela said.<br />塔里埃拉说：“(菲律宾海岸警卫队)将继续与所有相关政府机构密切合作，应对这些挑战，维护我们的海洋权利并保护我们的领海。”<br /><br />The Chinese embassy in Manila did not immediately reply to requests for comment.<br />中国驻马尼拉大使馆没有立即回复置评请求。<br /><br />China claims 90% of the South China Sea, overlapping with the exclusive economic zones of Vietnam, Malaysia, Brunei, Indonesia and the Philippines. Beijing seized the Scarborough Shoal in 2012 and forced fishermen from the Philippines to travel further for smaller catches.<br />中国对南中国海90%的海域声称拥有主权，与越南、马来西亚、文莱、印度尼西亚和菲律宾的专属经济区重叠。北京于2012年占领了斯卡伯勒浅滩即北京所称的黄岩岛，并迫使菲律宾渔民前往更远、渔获也较少的地方去捕鱼。<br /><br />Beijing allowed Filipino fishermen to return to the uninhabited shoal when bilateral relations were improving markedly under then-President Rodrigo Duterte. But tension has mounted again since his successor, Ferdinand Marcos Jr, took office last year.<br />当菲律宾前总统罗德里戈·杜特尔特(Rodrigo Duterte)领导下的双边关系显著改善时，北京允许菲律宾渔民返回无人居住的浅滩。但自从他的继任者小费迪南德·马科斯(Ferdinand Marcos Jr)去年上任以来，紧张局势再次加剧。<br /><br />Advertisement · Scroll to continue<br /><br />Philippine coast guard and fisheries bureau personnel discovered the floating barrier, estimated at 300 m (1,000 feet) long, on a routine patrol on Friday near the shoal, locally known as Bajo de Masinloc, Tarriela said.<br />塔里埃拉说，周五(9月22日)，菲律宾海岸警卫队和渔业局人员在该浅滩附近的例行巡逻中发现了这个大约300 米长的浮动障碍。<br /><br />Three Chinese coast guard rigid-hull inflatable boats and a Chinese maritime militia service boat installed the barrier when the Philippine vessel arrived, he said.<br />他说，当菲律宾船只抵达时，三艘中国海警硬体充气艇和一艘中国海上民兵勤务船安装了障碍。<br /><br />Filipino fishermen say China typically installs such barriers when they monitor a large number of fishermen in the area, Tarriela said.<br />塔里埃拉说，菲律宾渔民表示，中国在监视到该地区有大量渔民时通常会设置此类障碍。<br /><br />The Chinese boats issued 15 radio challenges and accused the Philippine ship and fishermen of violating international and China's laws, before moving away "upon realizing the presence of media personnel onboard the (Filipino) vessel", he said.<br />他说，中国船只发出了15次无线电挑战，指责菲律宾船只和渔民违反国际法和中国法律，然后“在意识到(菲律宾)船只上有媒体人员”后掉头离开。<br /><br /><em>(本文依据了路透社的报道。)</em></p><br><hr><div>获取更多RSS：<br><a href="https://feedx.net" style="color:orange" target="_blank">https://feedx.net</a> <br><a href="https://feedx.best" style="color:orange" target="_blank">https://feedx.best</a><br></div>
